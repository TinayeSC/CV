At HIS I enjoyed a range of Extra Curricular activities which helped develop me as a person. I attended multiple International Schools of Southen and Eastern Africa (ISSEA) sports tournaments and events. University of Bristol

At Bristol I studied a 1 year Foundation Programme in STEM. I passed all modules with results in the top half of my cohort. The modules comprised of Foundations of Physics, Mathematics for STEM, Accelerated Academic Language and Literacy, and Language and Communications. After passing with adequate results, I progressed onto Aerospace Engineering (MEng). The first year comprised of Engineering Mathematics, Fundamentals of Aerospace, Engineering by Design, and Engineering by Investigation. Unfortunately, I was not mentally healthy enough to succesffuly study on this course. I was given a validated Extenuating Circumstances but it was too late in the year for me to be re-enrolled on a repeat year. Although the year was difficult, I still felt that i learnt a few key engineering princples and it helped me to visualise my career path.
